# Model Improvement Ideas

This document captures potential improvements to the PlayeRank extended framework for future development.

## Feature Engineering

- тмЬ **Game context features**: Encode match state (score at time of action, minute of the match, home/away) as additional input features. A pass made while 1-0 up in the 85th minute carries different value than the same pass at 0-0 in the 10th minute.
- тмЬ **Opponent strength adjustment**: Weight player scores by the quality of the opponent. Scoring against a top team should be valued more than scoring against the weakest side.
- тЬЕ **Sequence/chain context**: Model multi-event chains (e.g. pass тЖТ dribble тЖТ shot) rather than isolated events. The contribution of each action in a chain leading to a goal is currently invisible to the model.

  **Implementation status:**

  1. тЬЕ **Chain detection** тАФ `chainFeatures._detect_chains()` sorts events by `(matchPeriod, eventSec)` and breaks chains on `INTERRUPTION`, `FOUL`, `OFFSIDE`, period change, or `teamId` change. Events with `playerId=0` or `teamId=0` are excluded.

  2. тЬЕ **Chain outcome classification** тАФ `chainFeatures._classify_chain()` returns `'goal'`, `'shot'`, or `None` based on the terminal event and `GOAL_TAG`.

  3. тЬЕ **Feature class `chainFeatures.py`** тАФ Extends `Feature`. Produces eight features per player/team per match:
     - Positive: `chain-shot-participant`, `chain-goal-participant`, `chain-final-action`
     - Harmful: `chain-turnover-precedes-shot`, `chain-turnover-precedes-goal`, `chain-turnover-final-actor`, `chain-conceded-shot`, `chain-conceded-goal`

  4. тЬЕ **Pipeline integration** тАФ Chain features are included in `compute_features_weight.py` (positive chain features only, via `featureFilters`) and `compute_lack_of_performance.py` (all chain features, split by filter).

  5. тЬЕ **README updates** тАФ Chain context model section added covering chain detection rules, positive features, harmful features, `chainScore`, `chainWasteScore`, and `chainNetScore`.

  6. тЬЕ **Dashboard updates** тАФ `тЫУя╕П Chain Analytics` tab added with chain score scatter, chain participation leaderboard and scatter, harmful chain leaderboard and scatter, and chain origin pitch heatmap. Player Profile tab extended with chain participation timeline.

  7. тЬЕ **Data volume** тАФ ~3.25 M events across 1,941 matches confirmed. `playerId=0` events (226,038 тАФ 7% of data) filtered at source in `chainFeatures.py` and at load time in the dashboard.

  8. тЬЕ **Chain pitch visualisation** тАФ Chain origin heatmap implemented in the Chain Analytics tab using `go.Histogram2dContour` on the existing `draw_soccer_field()` figure. `chain_map.json` generated by `compute_lack_of_performance.py`. Single chain replay, player contribution map, and chain length vs outcome views remain тмЬ not yet started.

- тмЬ **Minutes-played normalisation**: Divide per-match scores by actual minutes played rather than treating a 90-minute appearance the same as a 10-minute substitute appearance.

## Model Architecture

- тмЬ **Role-specific models**: Train separate SVM models for each role cluster rather than a single global model. Defensive actions are undervalued by a model trained on all positions simultaneously.
- тмЬ **Non-linear model**: Replace LinearSVC with a gradient-boosted tree or neural network to capture interaction effects between features (e.g. a high shot rate matters more when combined with high positioning quality).
- тмЬ **Uncertainty quantification**: Report a confidence interval around each player score, not just a point estimate. Players with few appearances should have wider intervals to prevent small-sample flukes from dominating leaderboards.

---

## Label Quality

- тмЬ **xG differential instead of match result**: Replace binary win/loss labels with the match-level expected goals differential. This provides a continuous, less noisy training signal and decouples luck (post shots, goalkeeper errors) from genuine team performance.
- тмЬ **Weighted match outcomes**: Down-weight heavily lopsided results (e.g. 5-0 wins) when training, since they may reflect opponent weakness more than individual quality.

---

## Aggregation and Ranking

- тмЬ **Time-decay weighting**: Apply an exponential decay so that recent matches contribute more to the current rating than matches from early in the season. Useful for tracking form.
- тмЬ **Plus-minus component**: Add a simple on/off plus-minus term (team goal difference when the player is on the field vs. off) to complement the action-based playerankScore.

## Waste Model

- тЬЕ **Independent feature sets**: `playerank/features/featureFilters.py` partitions quality features into non-overlapping performance and waste subsets:
  - **Performance Weighter** trains only on positive-outcome features (`accurate`, `assist`, `key pass`, `opportunity`, `counter attack`, `Free Kick-Penalty`) тАФ 34 features.
  - **Waste Weighter** trains only on negative-outcome features (`not accurate`, `yellow card`, `red card`, `second yellow card`, `dangerous ball lost`, `missed ball`, `Foul`, `Clearance`) тАФ 26 features.
  - Chain scores use disjoint subsets by design: `positive_chain` for `chainScore`, `harmful_chain` for `chainWasteScore`.
  - Verified: no feature appears in both subsets. Pre-implementation correlation between `playerankScore` and `wasteScore` was тИТ0.509 (weight-vector r = тИТ0.366). Re-run the pipeline to measure the post-implementation improvement.

- тмЬ **Asymmetric weighting**: Some action types matter more for waste than for performance (e.g. defensive errors). Allow the waste model to up-weight defensive event types relative to the performance model.

- тЬЕ **Harmful chain score (`chainWasteScore`)**: Implemented in full.

  **Implementation status:**

  1. тЬЕ **Two types of harmful chain** тАФ `chainFeatures._compute_entity_features()` detects:
     - *Type 1 (Turnover chains)*: chain[i] ends without a shot and chain[i+1] (different team) ends in shot/goal тЖТ credits all players in chain[i] with `chain-turnover-precedes-shot/goal` and the last actor with `chain-turnover-final-actor`.
     - *Type 2 (Conceded chains)*: opponent's chain ends in shot/goal тЖТ credits defending team players who had defensive events (`DUEL`, `OTHERS`) within the chain's time window with `chain-conceded-shot/goal`. Falls back to no attribution (rather than team ID) when no specific defender is found.

  2. тЬЕ **Training** тАФ `compute_harmful_chain_weights.py` trains `Weighter(label_type='l-wd')` on harmful chain features only (`filter_features(chains, 'harmful_chain')`). Output: `playerank/conf/harmful_chain_weights.json`.

  3. тЬЕ **Scoring** тАФ `compute_lack_of_performance.py` applies `harmful_chain_weights.json` to player-level harmful chain features тЖТ `chainWasteScore`.

  4. тЬЕ **Net chain score** тАФ `chainNetScore = chainScore тИТ chainWasteScore` computed in `compute_lack_of_performance.py` and written to `dashboard_data.csv`.

  5. тЬЕ **Independence from existing scores** тАФ Pre-implementation: `chainScore` тЖФ `chainWasteScore` r = тИТ0.891. Post-implementation the harmful chain Weighter trains on a disjoint feature set, so this correlation should drop substantially. Re-run the pipeline to confirm.

  6. тЬЕ **Dashboard updates** тАФ Chain Analytics tab includes `chainScore` vs `chainWasteScore` scatter, harmful chain leaderboard, turnover vs conceded scatter, and chain origin pitch heatmap. Harmful chain map (pitch overlay of turnover positions coloured by zone) remains тмЬ not yet started.
